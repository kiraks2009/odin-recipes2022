Pilau

Description

East African Pilau is a traditional, beautiful fragrant rice dish made with
many aromatic spices that adds an amazing depth of flavor to the rice.
It can be made with either beef or chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 5 potatoes medium - peeled and cut into quarters
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 Red onions medium - thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 tablespoons ginger minced
  • 1 serrano chile finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon pilau masala
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • ¼ cup fresh cilantro roughly chopped
  • 1 lb beef sirloin cubed
  • 3 Roma tomatoes diced
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • Salt to taste
